About Industrial Attachment Hub Center

We exist to bridge the gap between ambitious students and trusted organizations offering meaningful industrial attachment opportunities.

Our Mission

To provide a secure, transparent, and centralized platform that connects students to verified industrial attachment opportunities, ensuring every placement is credible, time-bound, and aligned with real industry needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading attachment and internship gateway in the region, where every student can confidently access verified openings and every company can efficiently reach future talent.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional attachment searches are fragmented, unverified, and vulnerable to scams. Students struggle to identify authentic opportunities, while companies lack a focused channel to reach serious candidates.

Industrial Attachment Hub Center centralizes verified opportunities, eliminating guesswork and reducing exposure to fraudulent offers.

Why Students Trust Us

  • Centralized access to active, time-bound vacancies.
  • Clear requirements, deadlines, and contact methods.
  • “✅ Verified Company” and “Verified Vacancy” badges for transparency.
  • Community-driven feedback through moderated reviews and testimonials.

Why Companies Choose Us

  • Targeted visibility among serious, attachment-ready students.
  • Efficient posting with a strict 14-day listing policy to keep roles fresh.
  • Brand credibility through verification and public company profiles.

Trust, Security & Verification

Security First: We prioritize authenticity and user protection.

Verification: Every company goes through admin review and must provide valid details before posting.

Verified Badges: Companies and vacancies that pass checks display a visible badge.

Zero Tolerance for Fraud: Any suspicious activity is investigated and removed. Users are encouraged to report concerns immediately.

How It Works

For Companies: Register → Verify → Post verified attachment vacancies.

For Students: Search → View details & badges → Contact companies directly via official channels.
